# IELTS Weekly Review

Use this at the end of each study week.

```markdown
## Week of [YYYY-MM-DD]

**Target band**: [overall / module]
**Study time completed**: [hours]
**Primary module**: [module]
**Support module**: [module]

### What improved
- [specific improvement]
- [specific improvement]

### What still breaks
- [recurring issue]
- [recurring issue]

### Rules that proved useful
1. [rule]
2. [rule]

### Rules to drop or revise
- [rule that did not help]

### Priority drills for next week
1. [drill]
2. [drill]
3. [drill]

### Retest plan
- [what to retest]
- [when to retest]
```

Review standard:

- Keep only rules that changed performance.
- Drop broad advice that cannot be executed during the test.
- Carry forward no more than three priority drills into the next week.
